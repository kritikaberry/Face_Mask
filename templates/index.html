About Mask It

We, a team of two, PySquad have improvised face detector to detect face-masks. In such unprecedented times, Mask It can be incorporated in systems to facilitate work.

Mask It provides:

  • Covid-19 Tracker
    •
  • Pandemic News Updates
    •
  • Face-Mask Detector
    •

On pressing the Face-Mask Detector, it enables webcam and shows the relevant output. Mask It only detects the surgical masks.The frameworks used are:

  • Flask as backend framework
    •
  • OpenCV to detect face-masks
